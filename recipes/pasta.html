Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a delicious homemade Pasta recipe that serves 8 people.

It's a hearty and satisfying dish perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked tri-color rotini pasta
  • 6 ounces pepperoni sausage, diced
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1 medium red onion, very thinly sliced and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • ¾ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 (6 ounce) can pitted black olives, drained
  • ¼ cup minced fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, pepperoni, provolone cheese, red onion, cucumber, green and red bell peppers, black olives, and parsley.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, sugar, salt, and black pepper. Pour over the pasta mixture and toss to coat.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow flavors to blend. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese before serving.
