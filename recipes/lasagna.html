Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a delicious homemade lasagna recipe that serves 8 people.

It's a hearty and satisfying dish perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and onion over medium heat.
  4. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with eggs and salt until well combined.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Layer half of the noodles on top of the sauce.
  9. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  10. Top with half of the mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Repeat layers in same order.
  12. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes or until golden brown on top.
